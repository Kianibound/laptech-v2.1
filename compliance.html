Laptech ISO9001: 2008

Since 1997 Laptech Precision Inc has been fully compliant and certified to the International ISO9001:2008 standard. The company became the first crystal manufacturer in Canada to achieve this certification.

Through its own high quality manufacturing facility and qualified suppliers, the company provides precision quartz crystals and oscillators to customers worldwide together with QCM and material fabrication to specialist industries.

Quality control and product development are continuous processes through which the company supports customers and evolving markets.

ISO14001: 2004

Laptech Precision Inc is committed to the protection of the environment and biodiversity through its manufacturing and supply activities. An active environmental management programme exists within the company and formal compliance to ISO14001: 2004 will be achieved in 2011.

Associated programes for RoHS compliance, waste disposal, recycling, and REACH compliance support the ISO14001 programme.

Restrictions

Laptech Precision Inc makes every effort to comply with all relevant manufacturing and supply standards including RoHS, REACH and SVHC. These standards may be applicable in the Laptech Precision Inc manufacturing facility Canada or at the group head office in the UK. It is not thought that any of the SVHC substances contained within the REACH criteria are used in the manufacture of or are part of the Laptech Precision Inc products.
